Welcome to the REB Legal Representative Knowledge Base. This resource supports legal professionals who sit on hospital Research Ethics Boards (REBs), providing structured guidance for new members joining the board and a reference hub for experienced members navigating complex protocol reviews.

Whether you are preparing for your first meeting, assessing an informed consent document, or looking up regulatory requirements under TCPS2 or ICH-GCP, this knowledge base has you covered.

## Getting Started

If you are new to the REB, follow these steps to get oriented quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the Introduction">
    Start with the [Introduction](/onboarding/introduction) to understand the purpose and mandate of the REB and where legal expertise fits in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your Role">
    Work through the [Role Overview](/onboarding/role-overview) and [REB Structure](/onboarding/reb-structure) pages to understand the board's composition and your specific responsibilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Study the Foundations">
    Read the [Legal Framework](/onboarding/legal-framework), [Ethical Principles](/onboarding/ethical-principles), and [Key Regulations](/onboarding/key-regulations) to build your foundational knowledge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare for Your First Meeting">
    Review [Meeting Preparation](/onboarding/meeting-prep), [Conflict of Interest](/onboarding/conflict-of-interest), and [Confidentiality](/onboarding/confidentiality) before attending your first REB session.
  </Step>
</Steps>
